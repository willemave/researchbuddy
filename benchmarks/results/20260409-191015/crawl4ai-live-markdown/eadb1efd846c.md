The era of fragmented AI experimentation is over; boards now demand deep AI adoption and measurable ROI. Carlos Gonzalez de Villaumbrosia, CEO at Product School, delivers a strategic blueprint for transforming product teams into a truly AI-native operating model. Moving beyond basic productivity hacks, Carlos explores the radical simplification required to build end-to-end applications at scale and how to survive the messy middle of AI implementation. Key Takeaways: – The Productivity J-Curve: Why AI pilots feel fun but production environments often lead to a temporary decline in productivity before reaching massive ROI. – From PM to AI Builder: Why traditional roles are converging and how Vibe-coding, Vibe-research, and Vibe-design are making technical barriers obsolete. – The One-Pizza Team: How AI agents (who eat tokens, not pizza) are shrinking team sizes while dramatically increasing output speed. – Strategic SaaS Revamp: Why Product School replaced or removed 66% of its own SaaS stack to force an AI-native workflow shift. – The AI Leadership Formula: A 4-part framework (Producer, Expert, Leader, Manager) to assess the quality of modern product executives.  - 🌪️ The AI Paradox: Trillions Vanish vs. Trillions Gained  - 📉 The Great Divide: 6.5 Billion People Left Behind  - 🧗 The Messy Middle and the Productivity J-Curve  - 🛠️ The Rise of the Builder: PMs, Designers, and Coders Converge  - 📊 Vive Research: Using Natural Language for Instant Insights  - 🍕 The One-Pizza Team: Why Agents Don't Eat Pepperoni  - 📈 The Declining CPO: Evolution into CPTO or GM  - 💎 The Value Stick: Customer Delight vs. Unit Economics  - 🤖 Agentic Growth: Optimizing Products for Bots, Not Humans  - 🤝 Hiring AI Natives: The Hard Lessons of Team Transformation  - 🏗️ Revamping the Stack: Why 66% of SAS Tools Had to Go  - 🚀 Scaling Output: Shipping 2 Years of Product in 6 Months  - 🎓 The Next Evolution: From Training to AI Transformation Partner Meet the Speaker: Carlos González de Villaumbrosia has over 10 years of experience building teams and digital products in the US, Europe, and Latin America. Carlos founded Product School in San Francisco in 2014. Today, the company is the global leader in Product training, trusted by Fortune 500 companies and a community of 2.5M+ professionals. Our expert-led, live, hands-on programs, run by AI-first product leaders, embed practical AI skills to help organizations innovate faster, experiment at scale, and achieve measurable business outcomes.
