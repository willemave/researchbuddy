Most software engineers don't see this coming. 👀 The US tech industry is quietly redefining what a ""great"" software engineer looks like — it's not the fastest coder in the room anymore. 🧠 AI is handling more of the execution. The new edge is judgment — knowing when to trust the model, when to override it, and how to shape its behavior for real business outcomes. John Lorance, Head of Product and Technology at Pyxos, calls them "context engineers." They write less code than you'd expect, yet they ship better outcomes than almost anyone.
