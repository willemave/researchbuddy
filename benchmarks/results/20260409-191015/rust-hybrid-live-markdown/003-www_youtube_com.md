# CEO at Product School | Beyond the Pilot: The AI-Native Product Operating Model

Channel: Product School

The era of fragmented AI experimentation is over; boards now demand deep AI adoption and measurable ROI. Carlos Gonzalez de Villaumbrosia, CEO at Product School, delivers a strategic blueprint for transforming product teams into a truly AI-native operating model. Moving beyond basic productivity hacks, Carlos explores the radical simplification required to build end-to-end applications at scale and how to survive the messy middle of AI implementation.

Key Takeaways:
– The Productivity J-Curve: Why AI pilots feel fun but production environments often lead to a temporary decline in productivity before reaching massive ROI.
– From PM to AI Builder: Why traditional roles are converging and how Vibe-coding, Vibe-research, and Vibe-design are making technical barriers obsolete.
– The One-Pizza Team: How AI agents (who eat tokens, not pizza) are shrinking team sizes while dramatically increasing output speed.
– Strategic SaaS Revamp: Why Product School replaced or removed 66% of its own SaaS stack to force an AI-native workflow shift.
– The AI Leadership Formula: A 4-part framework (Producer, Expert, Leader, Manager) to assess the quality of modern product executives.
