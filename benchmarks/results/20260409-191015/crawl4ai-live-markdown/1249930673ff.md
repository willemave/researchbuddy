## Beschreibung
vor 4 Monaten 
Welcome to a new episode of our podcast, in which we gain exciting insights into the world of setting goals with artificial intelligence! In this episode, I speak with Kathrin Falkenstein, a learning expert and OKR coach with over 18 years of experience in learning and development. We discuss: The challenges: How a dedicated team wanted to reduce its workload while strengthening AI adoption. The solution: The development of AI-supported prompts that simplify the OKR process and promote employee independence. The use of AI: How Kathrin uses AI as a sparring partner to achieve high-quality results. This episode offers valuable insights for managers, HR professionals and anyone who wants to effectively achieve their goals!
