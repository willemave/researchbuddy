## Podcast: Episode 174 - Your AI Isn’t Stalling. Your Org Chart Is.
[Michael Ianni-Palarchio](https://substack.com/@shapeoftomorrow)
Feb 27, 2026
Share
Transcript
This week on _Shape of Tomorrow_ , I open with a question that has been echoing through nearly every executive conversation I have had this quarter:
Why do our AI pilots succeed… yet our transformation keeps stalling?
The data is encouraging. AI usage is rising. Productivity gains are measurable. Agents are moving from novelty to infrastructure. And yet, inside most organizations, momentum slows the moment experimentation turns into scale.
Leaders keep searching for the constraint in the usual places. Data. Talent. Governance. Tools.
I believe they are looking in the wrong direction.
In Episode 174, I surface a structural blind spot that almost no one is willing to name out loud. It sits at the center of every organization. It has not meaningfully evolved in decades. And it may be the single biggest reason your AI ambition keeps colliding with reality.
This is not a conversation about technology selection. It is not a tactical playbook. It is a reframing.
If you are sensing that something deeper than tooling is holding your organization back, this episode will resonate. The leaders who win in the coming years will not simply deploy AI. They will rethink the architecture within which it operates.
Join me for Episode 174.
If you are here, you are not just experimenting with the future. You are serious about shaping it.
